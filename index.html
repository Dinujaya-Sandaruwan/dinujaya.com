Do you wanna know more about my story?

My name is Dinujaya, but you can call me Dinu. I recently completed my high school advanced level exam and now I'm eager to share my story as an aspiring web developer. During my time in high school, I got the opportunity to be a member of the school's ICT Crew. At that time, our team did a lot of work for the school, such as building the school website and an automated register marking system.
Being a part of the school's ICT Crew opened my eyes to the vast world of web development. Among the various technologies I explored, CSS resonated with me the most. I quickly developed a deep passion for CSS (SCSS), mastering it to such a degree that my fellow ICT Crew members recognized me as their go-to person for CSS-related guidance and instruction.
Around a year and a half ago, I discovered Mosh Hamedani, an esteemed expert in web development. Motivated to expand my skills, I invested in Mosh's premium courses, completing a comprehensive range of topics, including JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Node, Python, Docker, SQL, and GIT. This immersive learning experience allowed me to confidently label myself as a Full Stack Web Developer, equipped to tackle diverse challenges in this rapidly evolving field.
With my dedication and drive, I am excited to continue honing my skills and exploring new opportunities as a web developer.
